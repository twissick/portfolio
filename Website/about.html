About Us

Zenpo Translations was founded in 2019 by T.J. Wissick, a student of Kent State University’s world-renowned Translation Studies program. Prior to his time at KSU, T.J. lived for several years in Hokkaido, Japan, gaining a breadth of cultural knowledge that informs both the passion behind, and quality of, each translation.

Zenpo Translations is committed to you. We’re here to ensure that your carefully crafted documents remain just as powerful in your target language. Our commitment to excellence is like no other. Let us put that commitment to work for you.

No matter the project, Zenpo Translations is on the job. With intimate cultural knowledge and expertise in cutting-edge software including TRADOS 2017, our translation workflow is streamlined for peak quality and efficiency. Be sure to take a look at our portfolio page for samples of the world-class quality you can expect.
